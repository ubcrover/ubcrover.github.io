Who Are We?

We are a student-run design team from the University of British Columbia, dedicated to designing and building rovers to compete in international challenges.

Since our inception, we have strived to push the boundaries of student robotics, earning top placements at the University Rover Challenge (URC) and the Canadian International Rover Challenge (CIRC).
